I’m Bhrigu Bagga, an international student from New Delhi, India. My favorite Comms quote is "Communication is the solvent of all problems and is the foundation for personal development." By Peter Shepherd.

Alongside my studies, I work part-time as an international student assistant and Global Business Development Student Assistant at the International Division of Niagara College.

In 2022, I served as the executive advisor for communications and management of a prestigious cricket tournament in New Delhi. Additionally, I have experience working as the executive assistant to the Pro Vice-Chancellor after completing my Bachelor’s degree in Journalism and Mass Communication from Dev Sanskriti University in India.

As an executive assistant, I established the Digital Media Cell and represented the executive at the international relations office. I have gained valuable skills and insights from these experiences, and I am now seeking opportunities in higher education and the government sector.

As a student in India, I also had the opportunity to engage in two study-abroad semesters at the University of Vilnius and Vytautas Magnus University, Lithuania.
